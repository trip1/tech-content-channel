# Research Notes

## Sources to collect per topic

- Official docs
- Comparable YouTube videos and their titles/thumbnails
- Common Reddit/forum questions
- Real project constraints from the lab
- Screenshots, terminal output, diagrams, benchmarks

## Research checklist

- [ ] What question is the audience already asking?
- [ ] What mistake does this prevent?
- [ ] What visible result can be shown on screen?
- [ ] What is the contrarian or experience-based angle?
- [ ] What clip-worthy moment can come from this?
