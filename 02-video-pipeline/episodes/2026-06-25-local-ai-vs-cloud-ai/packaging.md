# Packaging: Local AI vs Cloud AI: What Is the Difference?

## Best title
Local AI vs Cloud AI: What Is the Difference?

## Core promise
A clear comparison of privacy, speed, cost, setup, quality, and when each choice makes sense.

## Audience
Privacy-conscious viewers and beginners hearing about local models.

## Viewer transformation
- **Before:** Curious, confused, or procrastinating on the topic.
- **After:** Has a clear first step, a decision rule, and a way to avoid the obvious mistake.

## Title options
1. Local AI vs Cloud AI: What Is the Difference?
2. I Tested This So You Do Not Have To
3. The Beginner Version of This Tech Trend
4. What Actually Matters About This Tech Trend
5. The Simple Test I Would Use Before Trusting This

## Thumbnail concepts
### Concept A: Simple scorecard
- Visual: dark terminal-style background with one big checkmark and one warning icon.
- Text: `WORTH IT?`
- Supporting element: screenshot/prop from the demo.

### Concept B: Before / after
- Visual: left side messy/confusing, right side clean checklist/result.
- Text: `START HERE`
- Supporting element: arrow from confusion to simple workflow.

### Concept C: One surprising rule
- Visual: face or cursor pointing at one highlighted line/checklist item.
- Text: `DON'T SKIP THIS`

## First 30 seconds
**0:00-0:10** — Show the demo/result or the scorecard immediately.
**0:10-0:20** — State the problem: viewers hear about this trend but do not know what is actually useful.
**0:20-0:30** — Promise the structure: one simple demo, one limitation, one recommendation.

## Open loops
1. Will the simple demo actually work?
2. What is the catch or failure mode?
3. Would I recommend this to a beginner?

## CTA
Ask viewers to comment with the next beginner tech workflow they want tested.
